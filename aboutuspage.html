How we started?

The main reason why we made this group is because all of our members have the same purposes. We all enjoy doing outdoor activities like camping, mountain biking, sailing in the sea, and enjoying the nature. All of our group members met in EDUSN school and we did a project together about outdoor activities. Then we became interested in that and decided to make this group in order to share about outdoor activities for the people who are interested in this field.We also put our group members' pictures, names and ages.
